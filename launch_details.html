Chandrayaan-3 Mission

Launch details

LVM3 M4, Chandrayaan-3 – Launch vehicle lifting off from
the second launch pad of SDSC-SHAR, Sriharikota Chandrayaan-3
waslaunched aboard an LVM3-M4 rocket on 14 July 2023, at 09:05 UTC
from Satish Dhawan Space Centre Second Launch Pad in Sriharikota,
Andhra Pradesh, India, entering an Earth parking orbit with a perigee
of 170 km (106 mi) and an apogee of 36,500 km (22,680 mi).
